# Prompt 01: Engineering Planning Document

You are acting as a Principal Software Architect, AI Systems Architect, and Product Engineering Lead.

I am building a QA Platform. Below is the research and recommendations gathered by another coding agent.

Your task is NOT to implement anything.

Analyze the research deeply and transform it into a complete engineering planning document.

Your output should include:

1. Product Vision
- What problem this QA Platform solves
- Target users and personas
- Core capabilities
- Expected workflows
- Success criteria

2. Functional Requirements
Define:
- User-facing features
- System capabilities
- Automation workflows
- AI/agent capabilities
- Reporting requirements
- Integration requirements

3. Non-Functional Requirements
Include:
- Scalability
- Reliability
- Security
- Performance
- Observability
- Maintainability
- Extensibility

4. Product Boundaries
Clearly define:
- What is included
- What is excluded
- Future expansion areas

5. Engineering Principles
Follow:
- Domain-driven design
- Clean architecture
- Agentic AI design principles
- Separation of concerns
- Modularity
- Testability
- Human-in-the-loop where required

6. Identify:
- Core domains
- Subsystems
- Services
- Major workflows
- Critical technical decisions

Create a planning document that can be used as the foundation for architecture design.

Research Input:
Use your research already did.
